## Chapters

1. **The Formalism: Hilbert Space, Dirac Notation, and Operators** — Translate a wave-mechanics problem into bra–ket form.
2. **Observables, Hermiticity, and the Spectral Theorem** — Justify why observables are Hermitian and find their spectra.
3. **Commutators, Compatibility, and the Generalized Uncertainty Principle** — Derive an uncertainty bound from a commutator.
4. **Quantum Dynamics: Time Evolution and the Pictures** — Evolve a state and switch between Schrödinger and Heisenberg pictures.
5. **Quantum Mechanics in Three Dimensions** — Separate the 3D Schrödinger equation and simulate orbitals.
6. **Angular Momentum** — Apply ladder operators to L; read the spherical harmonics.
7. **Spin and the Bloch Sphere** — Place any spin state on the Bloch sphere and simulate precession.
8. **Addition of Angular Momenta** — Build coupled states with Clebsch–Gordan coefficients.
9. **The Hydrogen Atom** — Solve hydrogen and simulate its radial and angular structure.
10. **Identical Particles and the Periodic Table** — Apply (anti)symmetrization; derive Pauli exclusion.
11. **Capstone — The Atom, Built from Simulations** — Assemble a multi-electron atom model and defend its structure.
