**The physics:** Hydrogen's Coulomb degeneracy places 3s, 3p, and 3d all at E = −13.6/9 = −1.511 eV. Screening in sodium lifts that degeneracy: 3s falls to −5.1 eV while 3p sits at −3.0 eV, a gap of Δ = 2.1 eV.
