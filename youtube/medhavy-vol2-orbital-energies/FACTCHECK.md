E_n = -13.6 eV / n²

3s, 3p, 3d are ALL degenerate at -1.511 eV (Coulomb degeneracy / SO(4) symmetry)

Source: Chapter 11. VERIFIED.

### Sodium n=3 splitting (experimental values)
Na 3s: E ≈ -5.1 eV  (first ionization energy of 3s orbital)
Na 3p: E ≈ -3.0 eV  (first ionization energy of 3p orbital)
Gap:  Δ = 5.1 - 3.0 = 2.1 eV

Source: NIST Atomic Spectra Database. Standard values used in textbooks.
The Slater approximation gives Z_eff(3s) ≈ 2.20 → E ≈ -13.6×2.20²/9 ≈ -7.3 eV.
The experimental values (-5.1, -3.0 eV) are the correct reference.

Note: exact numbers vary by source; the qualitative splitting is robust.
