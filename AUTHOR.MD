Medhavy AI, also known as Medhavi, is an AI-powered intelligent textbook system. The name comes from the Sanskrit word medhavy, meaning intelligent or intellectually brilliant. Medhavy turns static textbooks into living, AI-curated courses that can run alongside Canvas, combining automation with instructor control.

- Website: https://www.medhavy.com/
