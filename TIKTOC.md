## Concept
Volume 2 teaches the student to **work in Dirac notation, manipulate angular momentum and spin, and solve any central-force problem — culminating in hydrogen and the periodic table**, with the +1 simulation layer carried from Volume 1. Course textbook; chapter = week; read in sequence.

**Target course:** Second undergraduate QM course / first half of junior-year QM. 15-week map — chapters across Weeks 1–11, with the remaining weeks for the capstone project, review, and exam.

## The +1 Simulation System
Each chapter ends with an LLM Exercise block: the `CLAUDE.md` coding constitution, a copy-paste four-move D3 simulation prompt for the chapter's core object, exploration tasks, and an extension prompt. The Brutalist governing files are generated in Volume 1, Chapter 0 and travel with the student.

## Three-Act Arc
- Act One (1–4): the abstract state — Hilbert space, observables, commutators, dynamics.
- Act Two (5–8): angular momentum and spin, including addition.
- Act Three (9–11): hydrogen, identical particles, and the built atom.
